  • Chassis

    The Chassis team focuses on the mechanical aspect of our development platforms. They make sure our vehicles are safe and run efficiently.

  • Powertrain

    The Powertrain team is the most electrically inclined and focuses on power systems in our vehicles.

  • Sensing

    The Sensing team focuses on controls and sensing within our vehicles to keep them safe and operating at peak efficiency.

  • External

    The External team is in charge of reaching out to find funding and material resources and to show the world what we can do.
