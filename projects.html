  • Racing Kart

    The racing kart is our second development platform meant for testing our various research projects and eventually competing in the EV Grand Prix.

  • E Bike

    The E Bike was our club's first project and is meant to be a testbed for student projects. Any custom designed device such as a motor controller or battery management system can be tested on the bike.

  • REVO Bots

    REVO Bots is a class we teach on a yearly basis to educate anyone from Olin on the operation of electric vehicles. We distribute a kit to members here and make the kit part list available online. Classes are recorded and posted online as well.

  • Development

    We always have several projects under development. See a detailed technical overview of each of our projects.
